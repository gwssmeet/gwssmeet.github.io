GWSS Meet [About]

GWSS Meet is a basic website which lets students at GWSS connect with each other online. Find new names in your grade level or in your school and then catch up with them in-person or on your favourite social networks. Everything is 100% random - and you might just find your new BFF. Oh, and this isn't run by GWSS. It's run by a bored Garth Webb student. How did you get on this site? Maybe you were in one of my classes. Or someone told me to put you on here. Email me if you don't want to be on here or if you want to be on here!
